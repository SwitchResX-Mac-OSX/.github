## Overview  

**SwitchResX for Mac** is a powerful utility designed to give macOS users full control over their display settings. It allows customization of resolutions, color depth, refresh rates, and display arrangement, making it ideal for professionals using multi-monitor setups or ultrawide displays.  

With SwitchResX, you can create custom display configurations, manage multiple screens seamlessly, and quickly switch between resolutions without rebooting. It supports detailed control over external monitors, including those from LG, Dell, and other ultrawide or high-refresh-rate displays.  

The software integrates deeply with macOS, allowing users to automate display settings based on connected monitors or specific applications. It also provides detailed reports and logging of your display configuration history, ensuring optimal performance and easy troubleshooting.  

Whether you are a graphic designer, video editor, or gamer, **SwitchResX** enhances productivity by giving you precise control over every aspect of your display environment. Its lightweight footprint ensures smooth performance on both Intel and Apple Silicon (M1/M2) Macs.
